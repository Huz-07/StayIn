Nearby Attractions: Mumbai Travel Guide

Discover the best of Mumbai, right at your doorstep. Here are some must-visit places near STAY IN.

Gateway of India

An iconic monument built during the British Raj. Located on the waterfront, it's a popular gathering spot for tourists and locals alike.

Marine Drive

Known as the "Queen's Necklace," this scenic boulevard offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea, especially at sunset. Perfect for a relaxing walk.

Siddhivinayak Temple

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha. It is one of the most revered and visited temples in Mumbai.

Elephanta Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient rock-cut caves located on an island. Take a ferry from the Gateway of India to explore this historical site.

CSMVS Museum

Formerly the Prince of Wales Museum, this grand museum showcases a vast collection of ancient artifacts, art, and natural history exhibits.

Hanging Gardens

The Hanging Gardens of Mumbai, also known as Pherozeshah Mehta Gardens, are terraced gardens on Malabar Hill, offering views of the city and Arabian Sea, featuring lush greenery and animal-shaped hedges.
